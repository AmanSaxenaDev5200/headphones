About Us

Aspiring web developer that built this site!

Daniel likes to collect headphones as a hobby, but didn't have a way to easily keep track of his collection. Spreadsheets and text documents didn't display all the information he wanted and he wanted to be able to manage his collection in a simple way.

So came the idea for MyHeadphones!
